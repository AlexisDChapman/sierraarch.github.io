Zendaya

Zendaya is my hero. She is a very talented young woman who continues to inspire many girls and women around the world.

Here are some of the reasons why Zendaya is my hero:

  • She strives to be a positive role model everyday, especially for little kids.
  • She speaks out about sexism, racism and inequalit.y
  • She motivates women and girls to speak up for themselves and decrease injustice against women/girls.
  • She has stated that she would rather be remembered for being a good human being than being a great artist/actor.
  • She constantly reminds girls/women that we are all beautiful and need to be more positive about ourselves and others.

It is no secret that I am a big fan of women/girls who promote body positivity, self love and equality. Zendaya speaks out and fights for all 3 of those issues. She not only is a great artist/actor, but she is a great woman. She is the type of young woman I strive to be myself every day. I hope that all of the young girls and women out there take some advice from her.
