Dean Furness -To Overcome Challenges Stop Comparing Yourself to Others

  • Dean was paralyzed by a bale of hay and had to learn how to forget who he once was a normal walking person.
  • Dean had to learn to only compare himself to himself in order to find success.
  • Dean explains how it is very important that we take the time to focus on us instead of others.
