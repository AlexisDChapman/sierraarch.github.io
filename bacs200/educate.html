TED Talks

Dean Furness -To Overcome Challenges Stop Comparing Yourself to Others

  • Dean was paralyzed by a bale of hay and had to learn how to forget who he once was as normal walking person.
  • Dean had to learn to only compare himself to himself in order to find success.
  • Dean found his own personal success in wheel chair racing & only challenges himself against his past self instead of to his friends.
  • Dean explains how it is very important that we take the time to focus on us instead of others.

Noeline Kirabo -2 Questions to Uncover Your Passion--and Turn it into a Career

  • Noeline left a good job that paid well so she could follow her passion and turn it into a career.
  • She discovered that the world is constantly teaching us to look outward and to find a decent job and stick with it.
  • Noeline explains how it is important that we look inward and unlock our passions and find a way to make those apart of our daily lives.
  • 2 Questions to uncover your passion: If you had all the time and money in the world, what would you spend your time doing? & What truly makes you happy or give you a deep sense of fulfillment?

Dr. Ivan Joseph -The Skill of Self Confidence

  • He talks about how we need to build up people when they are doing good instead of knocking them down when they mess up.
  • We need to remember that people won't believe in us until we believe in ourselves.
  • Another key to self confidence is to interpret feedback the way you choose to.
Re-Imagining Work-RSA Animate
  • He talks about how we need to re-imagine how we create our workspaces. A lot of companies do open space plans which create stress/anxiety.
  • We should have the power to choose when and where we want to work.
